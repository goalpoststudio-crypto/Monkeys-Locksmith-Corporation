What we do

Every lock has a story. We carry the tools for all of them.

From a jammed apartment deadbolt to a full commercial master key system — here's exactly what Monkeys Locksmith handles, and how.

01

Residential Lockouts

Locked out of your apartment or house? We open the door using non-destructive entry techniques whenever possible, so your existing hardware stays intact.

  • Front door & deadbolt entry
  • Building & mailbox access
  • Broken key extraction
02

Rekeying & Lock Installation

Moved into a new place, lost a key, or just want better hardware? We rekey existing cylinders or install new locks, including smart and keyless systems.

  • Cylinder rekeying
  • Deadbolt & smart lock installs
  • High-security upgrades
03

Commercial Locksmithing

For storefronts, offices, and buildings — master key systems, panic hardware, and access control set up so the right people get in and nobody else does.

  • Master key systems
  • Storefront gates & panic bars
  • Access control & keypads
04

Automotive Locksmith

Locked out of your car or lost your only key? We cut and program replacement keys and fobs on site, for most makes and models.

  • Car key replacement & cutting
  • Fob programming
  • Ignition repair
05

Safes

Forgotten combination or a jammed mechanism — we open, repair, and service home and office safes without unnecessary damage.

  • Safe opening
  • Combination resets
  • Mechanism repair

Not sure which service you need? Just call — we'll figure it out on the phone.
